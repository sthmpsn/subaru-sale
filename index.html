About

I bought this car in May of 2017 from carvana.com (bought multiple cars from here with great success) for $18,000 with 29,660 miles on it. I was looking for something reasonably priced with AWD and low mileage and came across this gem. It's a fun, safe and practicle car but I will be relocating out of state and am looking to sell this instead of transporting it.

I work remotely from home so in the 2 years that i've owned it I only put 10,000 miles on it. I've upgraded the Stereo system to an Android system with GPS installed. Android comes with google maps and I use Waze but you can install just about any android app that you prefer. I also installed a mic for bluetooth connection and a backup camera that displays through the stereo screen.
